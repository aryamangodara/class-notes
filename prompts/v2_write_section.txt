Return an InteractiveSection: a `heading`, a short student-facing `spec_label`
(e.g. "Spec 8.5-8.6 · CP8 skills"), `covers_objective_codes`, and `blocks` — an
ordered list drawn from this widget vocabulary:

- prose: a SHORT paragraph (1-3 sentences) that sets up the widget(s) that follow.
  Keep prose minimal. Inline maths as \(...\).
- flip_cards: 3-6 recall cards (front term / back definition) for word-for-word
  definitions; bold the marking-point words in the back.
- table: a compact summary table (headers + rows).
- callout: one boxed note. kind = mistake (an examiner pet-peeve / common error AND its
  fix), formula (a key rule or shortcut), practical (a board Core-Practical box), tip,
  or remember. Include only when accurate and grounded; give a short title with an emoji.
- mcq: a quick check — a question and 2-4 options with EXACTLY ONE correct=true, and a
  distinct teaching explanation on EVERY option (why it is right, or why it is wrong).
- step_reveal: a worked example broken into ordered steps revealed one at a time, with a
  `think_hint` question first. Put the arithmetic of each step in its `formula`.
- sim: a live calculator. Give `inputs` (sliders: key/label/unit/min/max/step/default),
  `constants` (key/value — values MUST equal the REFERENCE DATA), and `expression`: a
  SAFE arithmetic formula over ONLY those keys (the keys, numbers, + - * / and
  parentheses; NO functions, no other names), e.g. "-(m*c*t)/1000/n". Optionally a
  `qline_template` using {{key}} tokens plus {{q}} and {{result}}, a matching
  `qline_expression` for {{q}}, and a `toggle` (a `factor` applied when ticked,
  e.g. 0.85 for heat loss).
- sort: a tap-to-bucket activity. Give `prompt` (the reaction/expression), `buckets`
  (key/label/accent), and `items` (label/value/correct_bucket) — values from REFERENCE DATA.
- toggle_diagram: template "energy_profile" with 2 labelled `states` (e.g. exothermic
  with product_position "below", dh_sign "−"; endothermic "above", "+"). The renderer
  draws the curve — you supply labelled states only.
- cycle_diagram: a Hess triangle — node labels top_left/top_right/bottom and the three
  `edges` (frm/to among those three, plus a label). The renderer draws the geometry.
- figure: a real fetched image — set diagram.kind = "image" and diagram.content to a
  SHORT 2-4 word search query (e.g. "coffee cup calorimeter"). Use for standard
  apparatus/photos; prefer toggle_diagram / cycle_diagram for energy profiles and cycles.
